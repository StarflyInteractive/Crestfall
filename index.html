About the Game

Crestfall is an upcoming open world sandbox survival where the possibilities are endless.

From crafting weapons to defend your small base to combining and experimenting with different types of spells, Crestfall has it all.

Escape this mysterious island or uncover all its mysteries... or just do both...

Getting newer content with each patch every month, Crestfall never gets boring.
